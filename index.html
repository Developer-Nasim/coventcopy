Got Q's?

Here are some of the most common questions we get from business owners like yourself.

How do your AI solutions differ from software?

We use proprietary methods to custom tailor AI models to your specific business needs using the data you give it. As such, our solutions greatly outperform market available solutions in every way possible whether it's for Creative Copy or Data Analysis.

What does your pricing look like?

Pricing varies greatly depending on the complexity and involvement required from us by you, for a customized quote book in a call.

How do you measure the ROI of your services?

This depends greatly on what the goal of the specific system is which we establish with you at the beginning of each sprint. Whether it's time saved, leads generated, conversions or some other form of result.

What does the timeline for delivery look like?

Timeline and Deliverables vary depending on complexity. We operate in 2 week sprints, so you can always expect a useable iteration of your solution within that time period.

What tools and technologies do you use?

We can integrate with your tech stack directly! Most commonly we use tools like Airtable, Make.com, Replit & more to accomplish our deliverables.
